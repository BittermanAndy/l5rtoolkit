Kensei
Legend of the Five Rings
Toolkit

The Kensei Legend of the Five Rings Toolkit is a set of Windows apps for enhancing your LCG and RPG games and other experiences in the Legend of the Five Rings setting.

To get started:

  1. Download the setup.exe and Kensei Legend of the Five Rings Toolkit.msi.
  2. Install the software by running the setup.exe with the .msi in the same folder.
  3. Explore the Extras folder to find additional files to use with the toolkit and in your games.

Read on to find out more!

L5R LCG Deck Builder

A deck list builder for the Legend of the Five Rings LCG.

  • Create, edit, save, import, and export deck lists for Legend of the Five Rings: The Card Game.
  • Support for Stronghold Format, Emerald Legacy, Obsidian, and most other widely-used formats.
  • Auto-filter available cards and validate decks as you go.
  • Randomly generate a deck, from scratch or partially filled in.
  • Import decks you’ve previously made in EmeraldDB, export for use in EmeraldDB and from there, online play in Jigoku Online.
  • Sample deck list files available in the Extras folder.

Coming soon (...?):

  • Print and/or export to PDF a pretty-looking deck list.
  • Shadowlands decks.
  • Filter available cards by your collection.
  • And more...?

Many thanks to the EmeraldDB team for making available the data used as the basis for this tool.

L5R LCG Deck Organizer

Organise the deck lists you created with the L5R LCG Deck Builder.

  • Manage all the decks you’ve created with L5R Deck Builder, choosing the folder where you’ll keep them.
  • Create new decks, see a summary, arrange into folders, move them around.
  • Import all public decks from EmeraldDB.
  • Sample deck list files available in the Extras folder.

Coming soon (...?):

  • Filter available decks by those that can be played with the cards in your collection.
  • And more...?

L5R LCG Card Viewer

An at-a-glance database of cards in the Legend of the Five Rings LCG.

  • Filter by format, cycle, pack, faction, deck, and type!
  • View any card and its stats.

Coming soon (...?):

  • Print cards for use as proxies.
  • And more...?

L5R RPG Dice

A dice and statistics app for the Legend of the Five Rings Roleplaying Game.

  • Simple statistics for making skill checks.
  • Choose your ring, skill, whether strife is acceptable, and whether you have assistance.
  • Compare the odds if you had more rings or skills - useful for deciding where to spend XP!

Coming soon (...?):

  • A dice roller, following all the game rules.
  • More helpful apps for use with the RPG, including a character creator, session tracker, and many others!
  • And more...?

L5R Fiction

A database of fiction written for the Legend of the Five Rings.

  • View a summary of each story.
  • For stories available online, links to read in your browser or download.
  • For stories available for sale, links to the store site.
  • Filter by publisher, author, and character.
  • Combine the story summaries for the characters you’re filtering by.

Coming soon (...?):

  • Improved information about each story.
  • Add and edit your own story information.
  • Visual flowchart so you can easily trace reading order.
  • And more...?

Many thanks to Kakita Kaori of The Winter Gardens of the Kakita for making available the data used as the basis for this tool.
